The iconic George Washington Bridge in New York City is a massive suspension bridge between Upper Manhattan and New Jersey. It is a landmark that is known for its distinctive towers, 
busiest traffic and status as a major transport artery. The George Washington Bridge carries major vehicular traffic of I-95/US 1-9/US 46 and provides the pedestrian/bike lane.
