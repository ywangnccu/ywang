INTRODUCING ALCOHOL FREE CHICKEN RECIPE

介绍无酒炒鸡



![INTRODUCING ALCOHOL FREE CHICKEN RECIPE](https://github.com/ywangnccu/ywang/blob/main/images/ALCOHOL_FREE_CHICKEN.jpg)

Ingredients

1 Lb chicken

1 Tablespoon sugar

1/3 Cup soy sauce

1 Tablespoon garlic

1 Tablespoon ketchup

1 Tablespoon vinegar

1/3 Cup fruit juice

Directions

Mix the juice, sugar, ketchup, garlic, vinegar and soy sauce together in a bowl.

Cut chicken to bite-size because it is the easiest to serve.

Sear the chicken in an oiled stockpot for about 9 minutes. Exact timing will depend on the size of your chicken pieces.

Pour the sauce mixture over the chicken. Reduce heat and let it simmer for about 15 minutes.

Finish with a cornstarch slurry that mixes 1 tablespoon cornstarch and 3 tablespoon cold broth to thicken.
