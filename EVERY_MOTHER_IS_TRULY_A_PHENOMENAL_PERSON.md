Every mom is truly a phenomenal person. She is the one that knows you even more than you know yourself. You can tell her everything. Your secrets will be safe with her. Even if sometimes it’s hard to tell, 
she is thinking of what is best for you. Mom has the experience. It is sometimes better than knowledge. She will never judge or ridicule, only listen and give the great advice. In good or bad times, she will never leave you alone. 
You can call her endless times and she will be more than happy to talk with you. She will never try to do something that could hurt you, and she will forgive you for almost everything. 
It doesn’t matter what you look like – your mom will accept you. She will support you and protect you. She cares more for you than she does for herself. She is the person you can count on the most and is trying to help you - she is even willing to give her life for yours. 
She’s the one that raised you. She’s known you and put up with you since your birth. She will be honest with you and she will make the greatest effort for you. The only thing she asks for in return is no harm. Your mom loves you more than anyone else could!

Your mom is willing to give an endless supply of support, respect, and kindness with a whole lot of patience and love that cheers you up. She is listening to your thoughts, 
teaching you how to love yourself, and being there for you any time takes a lot of effort. However, mom is your superhero, and if she manages to become your lifelong friend, this can be one of the greatest joys!

Mom is there for you. Even when you’re away from home, you know that you can call her any time. If you need help, she will try to come immediately to help you with your issues, as she is your biggest support during any of your life chapters.

Your mom loves you even when you find it hard to love yourself. Moments of breakdown might occur for different reasons - during these hard times, your mom will surround you with her support even more than usual. 
She will teach you again how to love yourself and will take care of your health.

Your mom worries about you, your life, your health, and your relationship. She might even think about your happiness more than about her own. 
She texts you and calls you several times just to find out that everything is good wherever you are, and no matter how old you get, your mom worries about you.

Your mom is your biggest fan. She’s believed in you, and sometimes does this even more than you do yourself. No matter what plans you have about your life, she will be there for you to remove your doubts and support you. 
The only thing she wants is for you to feel good, and she does everything to make that happen.

She is willing to listen to your information. When we have something good happening in our lives, we might want to share it with our whole family or our friends. 
However, the first person that gets to know everything is your precious mom - and she rejoices for you, sometimes even more than you expect.

Her advice is important to you, and so is yours to her. When you’re hesitant, the first person you turn to is your mom, because her thoughts and advice are crucial to you, and you take them very seriously. 
You’re not fearful of being honest about things that scare you, and you know that she will be interested in helping. Your mom might also ask for your advice, because she knows you can trust each other.

And you know you can be completely honest with her. You know that no matter what things are on your mind, good or bad — you can open up to her. She will try to understand you and won’t judge. She will keep everything a secret. 
As much as she is good at giving useful advice, she can also keep silent and simply listen when you need it.

Mom will tell you the truth, even if it’s sometimes uncomfortable to hear - about your current relationship, your outfit, or your lifestyle. When she feels she needs to speak up, she will do it. 
She listens to your opinion and tries to understand you. A conversation with your mom is a pleasure, because she wants to know your thoughts. She respects your opinion and doesn’t mind if it’s not the same as hers. 
She helps you to find the solution to a problem without changing you or your mind completely.

Your mom is an awesome person who knows a lot and also keeps up with the latest trends. You both feel just as good when spending time together, and mom never feels uncomfortable in your company!

Your relationship with mom only gets stronger year by year. With each year, you both learn to understand each other better, and this is what makes your relationship stronger. 
You feel how your love and respect toward each other grows every time, and this gives you a pleasant feeling - in the end, it might even become hard for you to imagine your life without your mom in it.
