Students at Eindhoven University of Technology in Juuka, Finland, have set upon a project in 2016. They were building a bridge based on an obscure model designed by Leonardo da Vinci in 1501. 
Eindhoven’s students opted to add a caveat to make their modern attempt extra-challenging. They built a 65-metre version of the bridge out of ice. The bridge was constructed from ice reinforced with paper fiber, making it strong enough to hold the weight of a car.
