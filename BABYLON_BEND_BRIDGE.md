INTRODUCING THE BABYLON BEND BRIDGE


![INTRODUCING THE BABYLON BEND BRIDGE](https://github.com/ywangnccu/ywang/blob/main/images/Babylon_Bend_Bridge.jpg)

The Babylon Bend Bridge is one of nine metal highway bridges in Fulton County, Illinois listed on the National Register of Historic Places. This bridge is over the Spoon River and is located along Illinois Route 123 near Ellisville.
